Live Markdown Preview

Markdown is a simplified markup language designed by John Gruber and specified in December 2004 with version 1.0.1. Aaron Swartz was the sole beta tester and played a significant role in the development of the syntax. One of the goals of Markdown is to provide an easily readable source form even before conversion.
